About

I am a Professor at the University of Electro-Communications (UEC), Tokyo, Japan. My research focuses on acoustic signal processing, spatial audio, and speech enhancement.

Recently, my research has focused on developing a system that performs wavefront synthesis in accordance with human motions captured by motion capture, and on investigating the perceptual effects as well as novel auditory user interfaces enabled by this approach.
